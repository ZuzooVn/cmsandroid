\subsection{Detailed Description}
This class encapsulates rotation. The duration of the rotation can be passed in the constructor and specifies the maximum time that the rotation animation should take. Past this time, the rotation is automatically moved to its final stage and compute\-Rotation\-Offset() will always return false to indicate that scrolling is over.
